BLACK TEA

5 : 19

  • Boil some water. Black tea is made with very hot water, so we recommend that you bring the water to a boil.
  • Add the tea. Pour the water over the tea to stir the leaves. It facilitates the infusion.
  • Intensification. Let the tea steep. You could sing the Marseillaise while you wait! ("long version" 5mn19s)
  • Remove the tea. Remove the tea ball or strain the loose leaves using a strainer. If you are using a tea bag, remove it with a teaspoon.
  • It's ready. Enjoy your tea, you are ready to face the day.
