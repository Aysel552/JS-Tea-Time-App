MASALA TEA

5 : 00

  • Bring the water + the spices to a boil: 1/3 tsp. teaspoon of cardamom seeds
    + 1/2 tsp. level teaspoon ground ginger + 1 cinnamon stick
    + 2 black peppercorns + 1 whole clove
  • Simmer over low heat for 5 minutes.
  • Add the milk and the sugar (sugar dosage at your convenience, but it must be sweet enough):
    bring to the boil then over low heat, add the black tea in a tea filter,
    let steep for about 5 minutes, over low heat.
  • Your masala chai must be tinted with a beautiful color reminiscent of milk jam.
  • Filter the spices before serving, very hot!
