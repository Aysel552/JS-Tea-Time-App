GREEN TEA

3 : 00

  • Boil some water, then pour it into your teapot and cups.
    Leave it for a while and then remove it.
    We call that "the announcement water", it allows not to subject a thermal shock to the tea.
  • Put the tea in the teapot and let it release its aromas into the steam of the ad water.
  • Return water to heat, but without boiling it, then pour it into the teapot.
    Ideal temperature for infusion: 70-80 degrees..
  • Leave to infuse, without stirring. The infusion is called “the exposure time”
    Ideal infusion time: 3 minutes.
    Do not leave it on any longer, as the drink will taste slightly bitter.
  • Remove the ad water from the cups and you can then serve it.
