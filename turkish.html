TURKISH TEA

10 : 00

  • Fill the lower part of the Turkish teapot with water and pour 1/2 or a glass of tea into the upper part of the teapot.
  • Place the upper part on the lower part and heat until boiling.
  • When the water is boiling, pour part over the tea in the upper part.
  • Then put the latter on the lower part and reheat over low heat to keep the water very hot. The tea is ready to be tasted when the tea leaves are well infused and they must fall to the bottom of the lower part (infusion time about 10 minutes)
  • You can now pour the infused tea from the upper part into a tea glass, mostly tulip-shaped.
  • You just have to sweeten at your convenience and enjoy tasting ...
