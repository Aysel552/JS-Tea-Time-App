  • Put a small teaspoon of matcha powder in a mug or in a chawan (traditional Japanese tea cup), then pour in about 60ml of hot water.
  • Using a chasen (matcha whisk), mix the powder with the water to avoid lumps. Stir for a minute , until many bubbles
    form on the surface of the tea and it is slightly frothy.
  • Your tea is ready, you just have to relax and enjoy it, perhaps with a little Japanese sweetness.
